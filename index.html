About

Hello! My name is Zoey, I specialize in print, interface/web design and commercial illustration. My passion is creating a strong visual balance between aesthetics and functionality (shapes and letters), which helps brands build a close human connection to their audience. Specifically, I am active in style of children illustration through which I want to spread calmness and simple everyday positivity by applying vintage graphics and neutral colors. In other words, finding the proper technique to visually and emotionally mix the comfort of reality and a realm of fantasy into one is, nevertheless, my favorite manner of storytelling.

If you want to work together for your collaborative project or have a freelance opportunity, I am happy to talk at hanhngoc9956@gmail.com.
